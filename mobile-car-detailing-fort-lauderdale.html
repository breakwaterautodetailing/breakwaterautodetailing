Detailing Built for Fort Lauderdale

Salt air off the Intracoastal. UV that doesn't quit. Drives that go from a marina to a parking garage to a humid driveway. Fort Lauderdale is uniquely tough on a vehicle's finish — and that's exactly what we're built for.

Why Fort Lauderdale Drivers Pick Breakwater

Fort Lauderdale isn't a one-environment city. You might keep your car in a downtown garage, park it at a Las Olas restaurant, drive across the 17th Street Causeway, and rinse it off after a beach day — all in 24 hours. Every one of those touchpoints leaves something on your finish: salt mist, brake dust, restaurant grease, sand, and sun. Without consistent care, that's how clearcoat fails early in South Florida.

Our mobile detailing packages are scaled for exactly this. Basic hand washes for the daily driver kept in a garage on East Las Olas. Premium clay + sealant for the convertible that lives near the beach. Two-step paint correction for the show car or daily-driven luxury vehicle that's been baking in the Coral Ridge sun for a few summers.

We come fully self-contained — water, power, and pro-grade products — to any home, condo, or office in Fort Lauderdale. You don't move the car. You don't lose half a day at a shop. We work; you go about your morning.
