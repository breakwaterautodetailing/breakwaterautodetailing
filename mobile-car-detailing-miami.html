Detailing Built for Miami

Miami is hard on cars in ways most cities aren't. Brake dust from stop-and-go on I-95 and the Dolphin Expressway. Construction grime from every block. Sea spray. Tropical sun that boils a dashboard. We take all of that off your vehicle — at your home, your office, or your building's parking garage.

Why Miami Drivers Pick Breakwater

The Miami driving environment is brutal in ways other places aren't. Your car gets baked in a Brickell garage all day, then driven through hot tar smell on the MacArthur. It catches construction dust on the way home. The salt mist off Biscayne Bay reaches a lot further inland than people think. And when it rains, it pours — leaving hard-water spots on every horizontal panel.

Our detailing packages are tuned for that punishment. Express details for the daily driver that needs a reset every few weeks. Premium with clay + sealant for the SUV that lives near the water in Coconut Grove or on Miami Beach. Two-step paint correction for the luxury car you'd rather drive than admire from the driveway.

We bring water, power, and pro products with us. No dropping the car at a shop, no waiting around. We work in your driveway, your garage, or your office parking lot.
