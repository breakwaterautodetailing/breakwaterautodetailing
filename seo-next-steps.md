# SEO Next Steps — Breakwater Auto Detailing

Post-launch checklist for maximizing search visibility and lead generation.

---

## Week 1: Launch & Verify

- [ ] Deploy the site to Netlify / Vercel / GitHub Pages
- [ ] Update all `canonical` URLs and OG URLs in every HTML file with the actual live domain
- [ ] Update `sitemap.xml` URLs with the live domain
- [ ] Submit the sitemap to **Google Search Console** → Sitemaps
- [ ] Submit the sitemap to **Bing Webmaster Tools**
- [ ] Complete the Google Business Profile setup (see `gbp-setup-checklist.md`)
- [ ] Verify domain ownership in Google Search Console
- [ ] Set up the contact form (replace `YOUR_FORM_ID` in `contact.html` with a real Formspree or Netlify Forms endpoint)
- [ ] Update the Google review link in `review.html` (replace `YOUR_PLACE_ID`)

## Week 2: Technical SEO

- [ ] Run **Google Lighthouse** on all pages — target 90+ for Performance, Accessibility, Best Practices, and SEO
- [ ] Run **PageSpeed Insights** and fix any flagged issues
- [ ] Check mobile rendering on iPhone Safari and Chrome Android
- [ ] Verify all internal links work (no 404s)
- [ ] Test the contact form end-to-end
- [ ] Ensure Google can index the site: search `site:breakwaterautodetailing.com` after a few days

## Month 1: Content & Citations

- [ ] Replace all `<!-- PLACEHOLDER -->` content in `about.html` with real copy
- [ ] Confirm business hours on `contact.html`
- [ ] Add real customer testimonials/reviews to the homepage (once available)
- [ ] Create **local business citations** on these directories:
  - [ ] Yelp
  - [ ] Facebook Business Page
  - [ ] Apple Maps / Apple Business Connect
  - [ ] Better Business Bureau (BBB)
  - [ ] Angi (formerly Angie's List)
  - [ ] Thumbtack
  - [ ] Nextdoor Business
  - [ ] YellowPages.com
  - [ ] MapQuest
  - Ensure **NAP consistency** (Name, Address, Phone) across all listings

## Month 2–3: Reviews & Content

- [ ] Actively collect Google reviews — text `review.html` link to every customer after service
- [ ] Target 10+ Google reviews in the first month
- [ ] Add a blog section (future enhancement) for content like:
  - "How Often Should You Detail Your Car in South Florida?"
  - "Ceramic Coating vs. Wax: What's Better for Florida Sun?"
  - "Top 5 Reasons to Get a Mobile Car Detail"
  - "How to Maintain Your Car's Paint Between Details"
- [ ] Add customer testimonials with photos to the site
- [ ] Update gallery with new work regularly

## Ongoing SEO Maintenance

- [ ] Post to Google Business Profile weekly (before/after photos)
- [ ] Respond to all reviews within 24 hours
- [ ] Add new photos to the gallery as work is completed
- [ ] Monitor Google Search Console for:
  - Crawl errors
  - Search performance (clicks, impressions, average position)
  - Mobile usability issues
- [ ] Track which keywords are driving traffic and optimize pages accordingly
- [ ] Consider Google Ads (Local Service Ads) once organic presence is established
