About Breakwater Auto Detailing

Locally owned. Fully mobile. Committed to making every vehicle shine across South Florida.

Our Story

Breakwater Auto Detailing was founded with a simple mission: bring professional-grade auto detailing directly to the people of South Florida. We believe that getting your car detailed shouldn't mean dropping it off somewhere and waiting — it should be convenient, effortless, and fit your schedule.

Based in the heart of Broward County, we serve customers throughout Fort Lauderdale, Hollywood, Pembroke Pines, Boca Raton, West Palm Beach, Miami, and everywhere in between. From daily drivers to luxury vehicles and classic cars, we treat every vehicle with the same meticulous care and attention to detail.

South Florida is uniquely tough on vehicles — coastal salt air, intense UV, pollen-heavy springs, and torrential summer rain all conspire against your paint. That's the gap we exist to fill: regular, professional-grade protection delivered on your driveway, on your schedule.

We're not a franchise or a chain. We're a locally owned business that takes pride in building relationships with our customers and earning their trust — one detail at a time.
